Scholarship

We’re aware that not everyone who is passionate about Node.js may be in a position to pay for a conference ticket.

With this in mind, we're delighted to announce the NodeConf London scholarship, which makes available a number of tickets at no cost.

Who's eligible

The scholarship is available to anyone not normally able to attend a conference.

Some of the groups we are especially keen to support include the following:

  • LGBTQIA+
  • Ethnic minorities
  • Women
  • Disabled people
  • Charity workers
  • Educational workers and students

How to Apply

Send us an email telling us a bit about:

  • Who you are.
  • What you do.
  • A few words detailing why you would like the scholarship.

Apply now

Please note that successful applicants will get a single standard conference ticket.

All emails will be treated in the strictest confidence, we will not announce who the scholarships go to but you’re welcome to say so yourself.

We are only able to cover the cost of the conference ticket so please consider your travel costs before applying.

Many thanks to Simon McManus for the inspiration.
